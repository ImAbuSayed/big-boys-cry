Pehlaan main ronda si kalla, now I walk away
Teri yaad bhul gaya haan main, living for today
Raftaar main fadda haan apni, shadows blur and fade
Tere jhuthe vaadeyaan ton bachda, escaping the shade
Aukhe ne raah saare mere, stepping on thorns
Seene vich agg lagi bhari, like fire burns
Mud ke na takda haan pichhe, changing the lane
Tainu main chhad ditta sajna, washing off the pain
